What are spongy bastards?

Neither a punk nor an ape but a bastard. The Spongy Bastards art collection has the cutest and most dangerous NFTs out there. Inspired by the movie Zombieland and Woody Harrelsons crazy crave for spongy yellow delicious bastards. Using generative code every Spongy Bastard is assembled randomly. All items included in the collection are handmade pixel art. Out of the 2,100 NFTs generated, many of the Spongy Bastards will have unique apparel, having a 1 in a 100,000 chance of being equipped. What is the Spongy Bastard Armory?

The Spongy Bastard Armory is a community driven NFT collection. A specific percentage of profit made from sales will be used to buy NFTs from other collections. The Spongy Bastard community will decide on what NFTs to buy and sell. Owning and verifying your Spongy Bastard will give voting privileges. 60% of the profit made will go back to the community.
