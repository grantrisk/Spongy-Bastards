What are spongy bastards?

Neither a punk nor an ape but a bastard. The Spongy Bastards art collection has the cutest and most dangerous NFTs out there. Using generative code every Spongy Bastard is assembled randomly. All items included in the collection are handmade pixel art. Out of the 2,000 NFTs generated, many of the Spongy Bastards will have unique apparel, having a 1 in a 1,000,000 chance of being equipped.
